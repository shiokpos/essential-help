Essential Help

Step-by-step guides for your Essential device. Tap a section below to get started.

Getting Started

Follow these steps when you first receive your Essential device.

Unboxing
1
Check everything in the box
Remove everything carefully and check the device and accessories are in good condition. If anything is missing or damaged, contact us immediately at support@shiokpos.com or WhatsApp +6011 1688 2880 before doing anything else.
2
Remove the battery seal
Open the back cover and remove the white tape from the battery. The device will not power on if this seal is still attached.

Fiuu Account Activation
3
Wait for your Fiuu approval email

Check your Spam or Junk folder if you don't see it. Contact us if it hasn't arrived after 7 working days.

4
Power on the device
Hold the power button on the side of the device for 3–5 seconds until the screen lights up. If it doesn't turn on, charge it for at least 30 minutes first.

Open Google Play Store

Taking Payments
6
Accepting a payment
From the home screen, select the payment method (Card, DuitNow QR, GrabPay, Touch 'n Go, ShopeePay, Boost, WeChat Pay, UnionPay, or Alipay+), enter the amount, and proceed. Always wait for the Payment Approved screen before completing the transaction.
Never hand over goods until you see the approval confirmation on screen.
7
Sending a digital receipt
The device does not print physical receipts. After a successful payment, go to Orders inside the app to send a digital receipt to your customer via email or WhatsApp.

1
Go to portal.fiuu.com

Device Care

Keeping your Essential device in good shape and what to do when something goes wrong.

Daily Use
1
Keep it charged
Plug in the device when not in use. New devices can take a few charge cycles to reach full battery efficiency — if fast drain continues after the first week, contact us.
2
Keep it connected
The device needs Wi-Fi or mobile data to process payments. For events or outdoor use, have a mobile hotspot ready as a backup. You can also insert your own SIM card for dedicated connectivity.
Troubleshooting
3
Device is frozen or unresponsive
Hold the power button for 5–10 seconds until the device shuts off. Wait 10 seconds, then power it back on. This resolves most freezing issues.
4
Screen shows "No Connection"
Check in this order:
  • SIM card is properly inserted and has an active data plan
  • Wi-Fi is connected under Settings → Wi-Fi
  • Restart the device and check again
  • Restart your router if the internet connection itself is the problem
5
Device won't turn on
First check that the battery seal has been removed from inside the back cover. Then charge for at least 30 minutes and try again. If it still won't power on, contact us.
6
Screen is cracked or device is damaged
Stop using the device if the screen is unreadable or damage affects its function. Take a photo of the damage and WhatsApp us with your merchant name. We will advise on repair or replacement.
7
Device is lost or stolen
Contact us immediately at +6011 1688 2880. We will help secure your account and advise the next steps.
Do not delay — report it as soon as possible to protect your merchant account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

Setup & Account
How long does activation take?

Usually 5–7 working days after we receive your business details. You will receive an approval email from Fiuu with your login credentials. We will also notify you when it's ready.

I didn't receive the approval email. What do I do?

Check your Spam or Junk folder first. If it's still not there after 7 working days, WhatsApp us and we'll follow up with Fiuu on your behalf.

Is Essential a POS system?

No. Essential is a payment acceptance device only. It does not include order management, menus, or inventory features.

Is there a monthly fee?

No. Essential is a one-time purchase. You own the device outright with no recurring fees from Shiok POS.

Can I change my bank account after setup?

Yes. Email Fiuu directly at support@fiuu.com with your Merchant ID and new bank account details.

Taking Payments
How do I know if a payment went through?

Wait for the Payment Approved screen on the device. Only complete the transaction after you see this. Never hand over goods based on a frozen or unclear screen.

What payment methods does Essential support?

Cards (Mastercard, Visa), DuitNow QR, GrabPay, Touch 'n Go eWallet, ShopeePay, Boost, WeChat Pay, UnionPay, and Alipay+. The exact methods available depend on what's enabled under your Fiuu merchant account.

Do I need Wi-Fi to accept payments?

Yes. The device needs internet to process payments — either Wi-Fi or mobile data via SIM card. Always have a backup hotspot ready for events.

A customer's QR code won't scan. What do I do?

Increase brightness on both screens, wipe the camera lens, and hold the device about 15–20 cm away. Ask the customer to refresh their QR code and try again.

Settlements & Records
When will I receive my payout?

Settlements are paid out T+1 working day to your registered bank account. Weekends and public holidays are not counted. You can track this under Settlements in the Fiuu Merchant Portal.

My expected settlement hasn't arrived. What do I do?

First confirm the settlement date has passed (counting working days only). If more than 5 working days have passed, WhatsApp us with your merchant name and the relevant dates. We will raise it with Fiuu directly.

Other
Can I use the device at different locations or events?

Yes, anywhere as long as you have internet access.

Can multiple staff use the same device?

Yes. Just keep the 4-digit passcode secure and only share it with trusted staff.

Can I send a payment link to a customer who isn't present?

Yes, but this requires Fiuu Online to be enabled. Your device runs on Fiuu Offline by default. Contact us to enable payment links — no extra documents needed from your side.

Still need help?

Message us with your merchant name and what you're seeing. We'll get back to you as soon as we can.
