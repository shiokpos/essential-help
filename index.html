Essential Help

Step-by-step guides for your Essential device. Tap a section below to get started.

Getting Started

Follow these steps when you first receive your Essential device.

Unboxing
1
Check everything in the box
Remove everything carefully and check the device and accessories are in good condition. If anything is missing or damaged, contact us immediately at support@shiokpos.com or WhatsApp +6014 3212 880 before doing anything else.
2
Remove the battery seal
Open the back cover and remove the white tape from the battery. The device will not power on if this seal is still attached.

Photo showing battery seal location inside back cover
Fiuu Account Activation
3
Wait for your Fiuu approval email
Activation takes 5–7 working days after we receive your business details. You will get an approval email from Fiuu with your Merchant ID, email, and password. Save these — you will need them to log in.
Check your Spam or Junk folder if you don't see it. Contact us if it hasn't arrived after 7 working days.
4
Power on the device
Hold the power button on the side of the device for 3–5 seconds until the screen lights up. If it doesn't turn on, charge it for at least 30 minutes first.

Your App

How to install, activate, and use the Fiuu Virtual Terminal app on your device.

Installing the App
1
Open Google Play Store
Sign in with your Google account on the device, then search for “Fiuu Virtual Terminal”.
2
Download and install
Select the correct app (published by Fiuu), tap Install, and wait for it to finish.
Add screenshot
Fiuu Virtual Terminal on Play Store
First-Time Setup
3
Allow camera and location access
When prompted, select “While using the app” for both camera and location. Then tap Activate.
4
Agree to Privacy Policy and scan your QR code
Tick the box to agree to Fiuu's Privacy Policy, then tap “Scan with QR Code”. Get the QR code from the Fiuu Merchant Portal under VT Activation.
5
Set your 4-digit passcode
Choose a passcode you will remember. You will need this every time you open the app. Once set, you are ready to accept payments.
Keep this passcode secure. Only share it with trusted staff.
Taking Payments
6
Accepting a payment
From the home screen, select the payment method (Card, DuitNow QR, GrabPay, Touch 'n Go, ShopeePay, Boost, WeChat Pay, UnionPay, or Alipay+), enter the amount, and proceed. Always wait for the Payment Approved screen before completing the transaction.
Never hand over goods until you see the approval confirmation on screen.
7
Sending a digital receipt
The device does not print physical receipts. After a successful payment, go to Orders inside the app to send a digital receipt to your customer.

Merchant Portal

Use the Fiuu Merchant Portal to check transactions, settlements, and manage your account.

Logging In
1
Go to portal.fiuu.com
Open your browser and go to portal.fiuu.com. Enter your Merchant ID, email address, and password from your Fiuu approval email. Tick the Captcha and click Sign In.
Checking Transactions
2
View your transaction history
On the left sidebar, click Transactions → Main Accounts. You will see a list of all transactions with filters for date, channel, and amount. Click any Transaction ID to see the full details.

Checking Settlements
3
View your settlement records
Click Settlements → Records on the sidebar. You will see all settlements with the date, bank account, and amount. Settlements are paid out T+1 working day to your registered bank account.

4
See which transactions were in a settlement
Click the i icon next to any settlement row to open the detailed breakdown. You can export the list as Excel, CSV, or TXT.
Activating Your Terminal
5
Get your VT Activation QR code
On the sidebar, click VT Activation. Find your terminal in the list and click View QR. Scan this QR code with your Essential device during app setup.
Managing Your Team
6
Give your staff or accountant portal access
Go to Settings → Access Settings and click Register Sub User. Fill in their name and email. They will receive login credentials without needing your main account password.

Device Care

Keeping your Essential device in good shape and what to do when something goes wrong.

Daily Use
1
Keep it charged
Plug in the device when not in use. Keep it away from direct sunlight and hot environments. If fast drain continues after the first week, contact us.
2
Keep it connected
The device needs Wi-Fi or mobile data to process payments. For events or outdoor use, have a mobile hotspot ready as a backup. You can also insert your own SIM card for dedicated connectivity.
Troubleshooting
3
Device is frozen or unresponsive
Hold the power button for 5–10 seconds until the device shuts off. Wait 10 seconds, then power it back on.
4
Screen shows “No Connection”
Check in this order:
  • SIM card is properly inserted and has an active data plan
  • Wi-Fi is connected under Settings → Wi-Fi
  • Restart the device and check again
  • Restart your router if the internet connection itself is the problem
5
Device is lost or stolen
Contact us immediately at +6014 3212 880. We will help secure your account and advise next steps.
Do not delay — report it as soon as possible to protect your merchant account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

Setup & Account
How long does activation take?

Usually 5–7 working days after we receive your business details. You will receive an approval email from Fiuu with your login credentials.

I didn't receive the approval email. What do I do?

Check your Spam or Junk folder first. If it's still not there after 7 working days, WhatsApp us and we'll follow up with Fiuu on your behalf.

Is Essential a POS system?

No. Essential is a payment acceptance device only. It does not include order management, menus, or inventory features.

Is there a monthly fee?

No. Essential is a one-time purchase. You own the device outright with no recurring fees from Shiok POS.

Taking Payments
How do I know if a payment went through?

Wait for the Payment Approved screen on the device. Only complete the transaction after you see this. Never hand over goods based on a frozen or unclear screen.

Do I need Wi-Fi to accept payments?

Yes. The device needs internet to process payments — either Wi-Fi or mobile data via SIM card. Always have a backup hotspot ready for events.

Settlements & Records
When will I receive my payout?

Settlements are paid out T+1 working day to your registered bank account. Weekends and public holidays are not counted. Track this under Settlements in the Fiuu Merchant Portal.

My expected settlement hasn't arrived. What do I do?

First confirm the settlement date has passed (counting working days only). If more than 5 working days have passed, WhatsApp us with your merchant name and the relevant dates.

Still need help?

Message us with your merchant name and what you're seeing. We'll get back to you as soon as we can.
